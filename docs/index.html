Find

FlowFinder helps you find local stream reaches, water bodies, and USGS stations. The app defaults to your current location, but allows for searches anywhere in the U.S. (using the OpenStreetMap geocoder).

View

FlowFinder let's you view five day forecasts for all of the ~2.7 million river reaches in the contiguous U.S. from the medium range NOAA National Water Model.

Download

FlowFinder allows all data, maps and plots to be downloaded as csv, rda, shapefile, html, or png where appropriate.

NOAA National Water Model

"The National Water Model (NWM) is a hydrologic model that simulates observed and forecast streamflow over the entire continental United States (CONUS). The NWM simulates the water cycle with mathematical representations of the different processes and how they fit together." Learn More.

Find unusually high flows. A basis for flood prediction.

FlowFinder highlights reaches predicted to experience unusally high flows compared to their long term monthly mean. It reports the location, peak flow, and local time while letting you vizualize the forecast.
